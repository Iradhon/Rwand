About Kicukiro

Kicukiro is a rapidly growing district in the southeastern part of Kigali, Rwanda. It is home to both urban and semi-rural areas, offering a mix of residential, commercial, and industrial spaces. The district includes key sectors like Masaka, Gatenga, and Gahanga, and is close to Kigali International Airport. Kicukiro boasts various educational, health, and recreational facilities, contributing to its vibrant community. It plays a crucial role in the economic and infrastructural development of Kigali.

Health Center

These include public health centers, referral hospitals, private clinics, and military medical institutions.

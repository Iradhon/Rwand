Administration Center

Nyarugenge is the central district of Kigali,hosting key government and administrative institutions.
The district is made up of 10 sectors: Nyarugenge, Nyamirambo, Rwezamenyo, Muhima, Kimisagara, Gitega, Kanyinya, Kigali, Mageragere, and Nyakabanda.

The district headquarters is located in Muhima Sector.

Administrative structure:

    District Mayor and Vice Mayors (in charge of economic and social affairs).
    Executive Secretary and Sector Executive Secretaries.
    Cell and village-level governance via Umudugudu leaders..

    Ministry of Justice, Supreme Court, National Public Prosecution Authority, and Office of the Ombudsman.

The administration of Nyarugenge District reflects a modern, participatory, and performance-oriented governance model. As the oldest and most central district in Kigali, it plays a vital role in national leadership, economic growth, and citizen service delivery.
